## Key Takeaways on Fuel Efficiency

**Most Efficient Overall:** Honda Odyssey RC4 Hybrid and Nissan Serena C27 e-POWER both achieve official figures of **3.8 L/100km** (74 UK mpg).

**Most Efficient Petrol-Only:** Nissan Serena C27 S-Hybrid at **5.8-6.0 L/100km**, though note this is a mild hybrid system.

**Worst Fuel Economy:** Nissan Elgrand 3.5L V6 and Toyota Alphard 3.5L V6 at **10-11 L/100km** officially, with real-world city consumption reaching **15-17 L/100km**.

**Real-World vs Official Gap:** Expect to achieve **30-50% higher consumption** than official JC08 figures in mixed real-world driving. Hybrids show the largest gap between official and real-world figures, particularly in highway driving where the electric assist provides less benefit.

**Best Value Efficiency:** Toyota Voxy/Noah R80 Hybrid offers excellent economy (**4.2 L/100km** official) in a practical package at a lower price point than larger alternatives.

---

*Note: UK mpg figures use Imperial gallons (1 Imperial gallon = 4.546 litres). Official figures are from Japanese JC08 or 10-15 test cycles, which typically show more optimistic results than European WLTP or real-world driving. Real-world figures compiled from owner reports across Japan, UK, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, and Malaysia.*
